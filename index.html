SPACssE

Hi! I am Ernest Czaplewski. After 40 days full of front-end learning I decided to test my actual skills and check what I can make withHTML, CSS and JavaScript. I wanted to create something more than just a landing-page, so I created a simple solar system that shows you how long it takes to pass a full year on each planet. The time is converted to hundredth of a seconds. For example the year on Earth is 3.65s. The solar system is fully interactive, so You can move all the planets in any order. The project is fully responsive so feel free to check it out via mobile and desktop. Farewell funfact: 1 year on Neptun is 165 year's on Earth so We can't overlive it.
